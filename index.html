Who is Banzoin Hakka?

Banzoin Hakka is a VTuber, someone who live streams video content and uses a digital character avatar that displays their facial expressions and body movement using motion capture technologies.

Like many VTubers, Hakka incorporates roleplay elements into his streams to depict a character. Hakka’s character is an exorcist who belongs to a group called Guild Tempus and lives in a fictional location called Xenokuni within the land of Elysium.

Hakka's fans love his humour and gregarious personality, his easy-going nature with his community, and his creativity and dedication.

Including Hakka, there are six active VTubers in Guild Tempus. Tempus is part of the Holostars English branch of the Japanese VTuber management and technology company Cover Corp.
